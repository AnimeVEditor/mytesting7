Heritage & Passion

A Culinary Journey to Old Kolkata

Rooted in the rich cultural heritage of Bengal, Kasturi Restaurant has been a pioneer in serving authentic traditional Bengali cuisine. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the exact blend of spices that evoke the true essence of a Zamindar household feast.

From the iconic Shorshe Ilish to the slow-cooked perfection of Kosha Mangsho, every dish is prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients and immense love. We don't just serve food; we serve memories of family, festivals, and the golden days of Old Kolkata.
