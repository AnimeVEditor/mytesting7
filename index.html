Our Heritage

A Tradition of
Taste & Hospitality

Step into Ramakrishna Lunch Home, where every meal is a celebration of authentic South Indian culinary heritage. For generations, we have been serving the bustling streets of Kolkata with recipes passed down through families, preserving the true essence of Udupi-style cooking.

From the crispiness of our morning Dosas to the comforting warmth of our traditional Filter Coffee served in brass dabarahs, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients. Our commitment to purity and traditional cooking methods ensures that every bite reminds you of home.
